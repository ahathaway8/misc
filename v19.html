Ford Interior Designer Anthony Prozzi brings a fresh perspective to car design. A native of Brooklyn, NY, he studied design at Pratt Institute School of Art & Design and worked for designer Donna Karan.

THE INSPIRATION

Anthony Prozzi cites Alexander McQueen, Elle magazine style director Kate Lanphear and jewelry designer Eddie Borgo as his inspirations. The car's chrome accents reflect these strong, edgy muses.
